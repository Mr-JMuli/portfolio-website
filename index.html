About Me

My expertise spans network administration, email systems, security policy development, and CCTV management. I provide user support and coordinate with vendors for seamless IT equipment procurement, configuration, and maintenance. Through continuous monitoring and evaluation, I ensure our systems are robust, efficient, and aligned with organizational goals. I take pride in a proactive, solution-oriented approach and clear communication with teams and stakeholders.

  • Security & Surveillance: Designing security policies and managing CCTV systems to protect assets.
  • User & Vendor Support: Providing IT support to users and collaborating with vendors for procurement and maintenance of equipment.
  • Monitoring & Evaluation: Continuously assessing systems to optimize performance and security.

I am passionate about exploring cloud computing, cybersecurity (especially blue team defense), Linux, and web development. I thrive on solving complex problems and leveraging emerging technologies to drive innovation and secure results.

My academic background (BSc) and multiple verified Credly badges further reflect my commitment to continuous learning and adapting to the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Looking ahead, I am excited to leverage my experience and skills to build resilient, innovative IT solutions.
